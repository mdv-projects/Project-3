SDI 1403: Hector Baldaguez

Project 3

My story consists of a one of a kind hunter that started from the bottom. His name is The BladeBow Hunter, and like his name he wields an unlikely weapon that consists of two weapons in one. It has an indestructible blade that can be wielded at the bottom and at the middle, and an indestructible string that can be used to trow an arrow with incredible power.

The Bladebow Hunter hunts people and monsters depending on the mission he chooses. He goes to the Mission board to get missions. He does 370 atk points per attack. There are three missions that catch his eye, one is of a Gunner called Gilbert the killer gunner. The second one is a monster called The Scarpler, and the third one is another monster called Nonsen the Blade.

He will hunt them to the end so he can get exp points to raise his level and become the most powerful hunter in the world.
